Welcome to Food Chain & Food Web in Animals

This site explores how animals interact in nature through food chains and food webs. Discover how energy flows from plants to herbivores, carnivores, and top predators, and how these relationships form complex networks in ecosystems.

Quick Facts

  • Food chains show a direct line of energy transfer between organisms.
  • Food webs illustrate how multiple food chains connect in an ecosystem.
  • Animals can be part of several food chains, making food webs complex.
  • Healthy food webs support biodiversity and ecosystem stability.
